About Nishuze

One random night, a friend sent a picture of a chappal brand called “Nishu” into our Discord group to make fun of my nickname (Nishu). What began as a dumb meme slowly turned into sketches, ideas, late-night designs, and eventually a real brand. We realized something powerful. The best brands don’t always start in boardrooms. Sometimes they start in group chats.

We source premium leathers and textiles from certified artisans, ensuring every stitch carries the weight of intention and the lightness of mastery.
