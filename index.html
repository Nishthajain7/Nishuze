About Nishuze

Founded in 2026, Nishuze began as a small project in Hyderabad dedicated to the belief that footwear should be as bold as the person wearing it. Each pair is designed with obsessive attention to material, form, and feel.

We source premium leathers and textiles from certified artisans, ensuring every stitch carries the weight of intention and the lightness of mastery.
